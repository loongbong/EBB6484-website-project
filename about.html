Meet Your Tutor at Kuching English Tutoring: Ning Bong

Passion for Teaching

Ning is deeply passionate about teaching, viewing it as a dynamic field ripe for innovation and exploration. She strongly advocates for student-centred blended learning approaches.

Extensive Experience

With tutoring experience spanning China, Australia, and Malaysia since 2007, Ning began her formal career as an English teacher in 2016. Since January 2016, she has been a dedicated teacher at Lodge International School, specializing in Cambridge IGCSE First Language English and Cambridge GCE A-Level English Language. Previously, she tutored SPM English, Biology, and Chemistry, and taught public speaking to younger students.

Proven Results

Ning has a strong track record, guiding an average of 70% of her IGCSE First Language English students to A/A* grades over four years. She successfully adapted to the 2021 GCE A-Level English Language syllabus changes, helping over 30% of her students achieve an A grade, significantly above the world average.

Qualifications & Skills

Ning holds a Cambridge International Diploma in Teaching and Learning (CIDTL) and a Bachelor of Biomedical Science (Honours) from the University of Western Australia. She is fully bilingual in English and Mandarin Chinese, possesses excellent communication and technological skills, and has experience in mentoring colleagues and curriculum development.
